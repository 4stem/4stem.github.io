$$ P_B + \frac{1}{2}ρv_Β^2+ρgH=P_Γ+\frac{1}{2}ρv_Γ^2+ρgh\overset{P_Β=P_Γ=P_{at}}{\underset{v_Β=0}{\implies}} \\\      ρg5h=\frac{1}{2}ρv_Γ^2+ρgh\implies v_Γ^2=8gh\implies v_Γ=2\sqrt{2gh}$$
